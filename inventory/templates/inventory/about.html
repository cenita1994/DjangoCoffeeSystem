About Django Coffee System

A simple Django-based inventory management web application.

Django Coffee System is designed to help users manage coffee shop product inventory records through a simple web-based interface.

The system allows users to add, view, edit, and delete product information using Django, SQLite, Model Forms, and CRUD operations.
